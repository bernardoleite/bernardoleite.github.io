Overview

From an early age, I have been passionate about computers and how they operate (after cleaning many viruses!). Today, I am a motivated researcher and professional in Computer Science, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Education Technology, and Question Generation -- the focus of my PhD research, which is expected to be completed this year (2025). I am open to new opportunities and collaborations. Please feel free to contact me at bernardo.leite@fe.up.pt.
